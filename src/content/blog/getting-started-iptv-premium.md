Setting up IPTV Premium is quick and straightforward. This guide walks you through every step from purchase to watching your first channel.

## Step 1: Choose Your Plan

Select either **Eagle 4K ($25/year)** or **Strong 8K ($40/year)**. Both plans include 1 year of Hot Player activation with no hidden fees.

- Eagle 4K: 20,000+ channels, HD & 4K quality
- Strong 8K: 25,000+ channels, 8K & 4K, priority support

## Step 2: Contact Us on WhatsApp

After purchasing, reach out to us on WhatsApp with your order details. Our support team will activate your subscription within 5 minutes.

## Step 3: Install Hot Player

Download Hot Player from your device's app store:

- **Smart TV:** Samsung, LG, Sony Smart Hub
- **Android:** Google Play Store
- **iOS:** Apple App Store
- **Fire Stick:** Amazon Appstore

## Step 4: Log In and Enjoy

Open Hot Player, enter the credentials provided by our team, and start watching. Your subscription works on multiple devices simultaneously.
